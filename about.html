Jules Mugabo

Photographer

A great photograph is one that fully expresses what one feels, in the deepest sense, about what is being photographed. -Ansel Adams

Background

Jules Mugabo is an aspiring photographer based in Kigali,Rwanda. He has n keen eye for details and his passion enables him to capture pics that are natural and aesthetic. His interpersonal and communication skills help in the interaction with his clients and therefore their needs and preferences are taken into much consideration. Jules Mugabo's artistic skills also enabled him to have his own studio "Legendary Studioz".

Styles

1.Portrait photography: This style focuses on capturing people, often in posed or staged settings. The goal is to highlight the subject's personality and features, using techniques such as lighting and composition.

2.Landscape photography: This style focuses on capturing beautiful outdoor scenes, such as mountains, forests, and oceans. It often involves using wide-angle lenses and capturing the natural light of the environment.

3.Street photography: This style involves capturing candid moments of everyday life in public places, often in urban environments. It requires quick reflexes and the ability to capture a moment in time.

4.Fine art photography: This style involves creating images that are meant to be viewed as art, often using abstract or surreal techniques. It may involve manipulating the image in post-processing to achieve a specific effect.
